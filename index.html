About nature

The local nature is modest and generous at the same time.
Most of the territory of the Pavlodar Irtysh region is flat boundless steppes, where it is easy and free to breathe, where it is spacious to the eye, and the soul is joyful.
In the north - a corner of the forest-steppe with shy birch pegs.

Weather

The climate in Pavlodar is considered moderate.
But in some cases it may seem harsh. For example, the weather in Pavlodar is famous for being one of the few cities where you can find snow cover in the winter season.
The air temperature does not exceed -25 during the day, and -27 at night.
